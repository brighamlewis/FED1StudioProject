Leadership

John Talcott, Company President

One of the three founders of chef's toolkit John found his passion in cooking at a young age when he began cooking with family at a young age. Cooking throughout his entire life John decided to pursue a career as a chef. After successfully graduating from culinary school John worked in the restaurant industry for ten years. Him and some of his friends from culinary school, Jessica and Jim, Decided the way they were taught was quickly becoming ineffecient and dated. They decided to start their own cooking programs and named it Chef's Toolkit.

Jessica Platt

Cofounder of Chef's Toolkit. Jessica began cooking when she was three with kitchen playsets, Ever since then you can always find her at home cooking. Her mother taught her how to cook and she decided along with John and Jim that she wanted to spread the love of cooking around her community through courses and lessons.

Jim Haliday

One of the Cofounders of Chef's Toolkit, Jim has had a passion for cooking since his teens when he used it as a hobby to stay out of trouble. He attended culinary school with John and Jessica and forged a friendship with them. They used this bond to help spread their knowledge through chef's toolkit.
