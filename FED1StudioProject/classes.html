Class Subjects

Beginner Cooking Applications

This class is a very beginner friendly and focused. We will look at the basics of cooking as well as preparing dishes that are perfect for family nights but still sophisticated and delicious.

Beginner Cooking Theory

A dive into the theory of cooking in order to learn why cooks do certain things in certain ways with the goal to further your culinary understanding.

Intermediate Cooking Applications

An intro into more complex and difficult cooking styles and techniques used by professional chefs to elevate their meals.

Intermediate Cooking Theory

Focuses on flavors and palettes in order to create a deep understaning in culinary arts.

Advanced Cooking Application

Restaurant based cooking techniques including how to work in a professional kitchen as well as techniques to keep the kitchen running perfectly.

Advanced Cooking Theory

Focuses on flavors and palettes common in higher level dining and tasting menus to give a nearly full understanding of food and flavors to create an amazing chef.
