About Us

Cook's Toolkit was originally started as a way a group of friends could hold a few classes within their community in order to help teach and spread their passion, Cooking. The educational program has since then expanded tenfold, We are now offering various courses ranging from beginner to advanced in order to help make you the cook you have always wanted to be. Whether its being the perfect home cook or looking to carve your way into the restaurant industry we are the perfect launchpad that will get your dreams cooking in no time.


Why Choose Us

Practical and Informative Teaching Styles

Do ever feel like traditional teaching are excessively dated and unnecessary? That is where Cook's Toolbox is innovating. We strive to bring you the best and most effecient student experience possible. We dont muddle around with tests and homework. We believe that the most effective style of teaching, especially in the restaurant industry, is experience and hands on learning.

Work Around YOUR Schedule

Most of our students are normal working adults with families and other commitments that need to be upheld. This is why we have an convenient and realistic schedule that is flexible with the life of a working adult. We pride ourselves in lowering the barrier of entry into this wonderful profession and hobby. When we started Chef's Toolkit our vision was teaching our neighbors and community the joys of cooking within their schedule.
